About WRO Advisory

We are a premium executive advisory firm founded on the belief that the right advice, delivered by experienced practitioners, changes the trajectory of organizations.

Our Story

Why WRO Advisory Exists

WRO Advisory was founded by senior executives who saw a gap in the advisory market: too many consultants who had never run a business, offering advice they had never had to implement themselves.

We built WRO Advisory to be fundamentally different. Every advisor we field has held executive accountability — from C-suite roles and board positions to leading major transformations in complex organizations.

The result is advice that is not just insightful, but executable. We don't hand over a report and walk away. We stand alongside your leadership through strategy and into delivery.

Our Approach

Practitioner-Led Advisory

WRO Advisory advisors have held P&L responsibility, led enterprise-wide transformations, built and sold businesses, and sat at the board table. We don't theorize — we draw from lived experience.

Each engagement begins with listening. We take time to understand your organization's history, culture, stakeholder landscape, and strategic ambitions before offering a single recommendation.

  • Rigorous diagnostic to establish ground truth before strategy
  • Co-created roadmaps with your leadership team, not handed down from above
  • Embedded support through implementation — not just the planning phase
  • Regular governance checkpoints to keep execution on track
