Faculty Grievance & Career Transition

Ontario's public colleges are cutting programs and laying off faculty at a scale the sector has never seen. Since the 2024 federal cap on international student permits, more than 600 programs have been suspended and close to 10,000 positions cut or projected across the province's 24 colleges.

WRO Advisory helps full-time faculty facing layoff build a grievance case — and a career transition plan — grounded in the full scope of their competence, skills, and experience, not just their most recent course load. We bring senior labour relations and HR expertise from both sides of the table: NLRB investigation, management-side negotiation against a national union, and union-side HR leadership.

Grievance readiness assessment
Resume and competency portfolio development
Union representation gap analysis
Arbitration-ready documentation and evidence organization
Career transition and resume strategy
Confidential, no-obligation initial review
