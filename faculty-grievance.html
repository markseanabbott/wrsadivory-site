Ontario's public colleges are cutting programs and laying off faculty at a scale the sector has never seen. WRO Advisory helps full-time faculty build a grievance case grounded in their full competence, skills, and experience — not just their most recent course load.

The Situation

A Crisis Faculty Didn't Create

Since the 2024 federal cap on international student permits, more than 600 programs have been suspended and close to 10,000 positions cut or projected across Ontario's 24 public colleges. Layoffs are moving fast, and the standard arbitration test — competence, skills, and experience — is being applied inconsistently.

Most faculty are walking into the most consequential professional process of their careers without an independent read on whether their case is as strong as it needs to be. Your union has dozens or hundreds of members to manage. Your case needs to be yours.
